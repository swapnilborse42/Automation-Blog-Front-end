What is Cognitive Automation and its key areas

Credit: Google

Cognitive Automation is explained by individuals to uncover and transform the hidden or “Dark Data” to automate marketing methods end to end security and also efficiently while together eliminating human error.

Information workers are no longer needed to extract knowledge from unstructured content- reports, Pictures, emails, they are free to do what they do much -make decisions and handle the exclusion.

Cognitive Bot holds getting from corrections made by your knowledge workers, getting smarter and more accurate over time. Cognitive automation implemented to achieve end-end process automation.

A standardised cognitive tool should consist of the below points:

  • Enrich the Data
  • Training the Document
  • Validation
  • Document Analysis
  • Preprocessing
  • ICR/OCR Usage

What does cognitive automation mean for the enterprise?

The bellow factors make cognitive automation next big enhancement for enterprise-level operations

  1. Cost-effective

    Cognitive automation can help companies to save up to 50% of their total spending for FTE, and other related costs.

  2. Operational Efficiency

    Automation can enhance employee productivity, leading to better operational efficiency.

  3. Increased accuracy

    Such systems are able to derive meaningful predictions from a vast repository of structured and unstructured data with impeccable accuracy.

  4. Facts-based decision making

    Strategic business decisions drill down to facts and experiences. Merging both, cognitive systems offer next level competencies for important decision making.

Enterprise challenges and the benefits of cognitive automation

Today's business faces many challenges: increasing efficiency, decision making, being competitive, ensuring customer loyalty, and some of the obstacles that compliance businesses face.

Cognitive automation has determined to be effective in directing those key challenges by assisting companies in optimizing their entire business along with their daily operations.

Based on our experience, we believe companies can expect more than 50% savings on FTE operations and associated cost reductions.

The advantages of cognitive automation include developments in compliance and overall business status, more comprehensive operational scalability, decreased turnaround, and below error measures. Each of these has a positive impact on business comfort and employee efficiency.

Applications of cognitive automation

  1. End-to-end customer assistance

    Industries can understand their consumer journey and recognise the interactions where automation can improve. For instance, Religare - a leading health insurance company incorporated NLP-powered chatbot into their services and automated their customer-support and achieved almost 80% FTE savings. Methods like policy renewal, customer query ticket management, handling general customer queries at scale, etc. are possible for the company through chatbots.

  2. Claims processing

    Under insurance, claims agreement is a huge challenge as it requires reviewing policy documents, coverage, the legality of insured components, fraud analytics, and more. Cognitive systems provide making automated decisions in seconds by analyzing all the claims parameters in real-time.
