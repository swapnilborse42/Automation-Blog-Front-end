Marketing Automation Tools You Need to Use

Automation Marketing is a part of Customer Relationship Management (CRM). There are various marketing automation tools plus platforms available in the market to automate these marketing tasks.

Constant Contact

Constant Contact is a very useful and powerful email-marketing automation tool that has some unique pieces to take your marketing exceeding only in basics.

It proposes Facebook fan advertising, coupons and deals, and event control.

You can also edit emails with the tool, use an autoresponder, and more.

The cost for 0 to 500 contacts is $20 per month for just email marketing and $45 per month for access to all features.

For 501 to 2,500 contacts, email marketing is $45 per month. All features are $70 each month.

2,501 to 5,000 contacts costs $65 every month for email marketing and $95 per month for full access to features.

Email marketing for 5,001 to 10,000 contacts is $95 per month, while all features for the same amount of customers is $125 per month.

HubSpot

HubSpot is a Software Company for Inbound Marketing and Sales. It is launched in 2006 and is headquartered at Massachusetts, United States. The company had revenue of $271 million in 2016 with 34,000 customers across 90 countries and 3,400 agency partners.

Hubspot is specifically designed considering small and medium-sized businesses.

HubSpot provides marketing tools for web analytics, landing page, social media marketing, search engine optimization, content management, etc. It is an Inbound Marketing Software that helps to launch marketing campaigns in a short time but in an effective manner.

AdRoll

AdRoll is helpful for retargeting clients or buyers through re-engagement on Facebook, Twitter, web, or social media.

Including cross-device and cross-platform retargeting abilities as well as flexible segmentation, you can produce customized experiences that dramatically improve marketing performance.

Key features include retargeting in devices and platforms, dynamic LiquidAds, flexible segmentation, transparent analytics, and expert optimization and conversion reporting.

SendinBlue

It requires no specific skill to create email designs or templates. The interface is so simple to use with a drag and drop facility and customized template gallery which helps to create a simple but unique email template. This email template is supported by all types of mobile devices with any version.

SendinBlue offers various plans and the lowest one starts at $7.37 per month. This plan includes unlimited contacts and 40,000 emails.

Bremy

A Solution Dynamics product, Bremy offers online multi-channel publishing assistance and services.

Bremy offers to configure a customized digital combination of email newsletters, database publishing, video proofing, and more.

Key highlights include streamlined video proofing including multi-user collaboration, advanced digital-asset control, and streamlined publication work and modification.

LeadSquared

LeadSquared was established in 2012 with a revenue of $3 million during 2016.

It offers a solution in areas such as real estate, education, travel, health, financial services, etc. It provides clients like Flipkart, Time Pro, NIIT, Randstad, etc. LeadSquared provides services in almost 20 countries.

LeadSquare focuses assistance in the B2C model i.e. Business to Consumer service in this model results or services, are instantly sold to the customer.

BuzzBuilder Pro

BuzzBuilder Pro is beneficial for entrepreneurs, sales teams, and marketers. Find prospects, create leads, and nurture them more easily.

Key features offer advanced email-marketing automation, a website design builder, tracking and analytics, lead nurturing, scoring and improving social media marketing, and hot-lead warnings.

ConvertKit

ConvertKit is the most simplistic marketing automation tool. Designed essentially for bloggers and content creators it’s a magnificent tool for beginners looking for necessary marketing automation software.

Do not demand any bells and whistles - ConvertKit doesn’t still have email templates, as they inspire users to transfer plain-text emails to improve engagement.
