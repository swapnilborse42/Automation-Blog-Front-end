Top 10 companies in the automation industry

Top 10 companies in the automation industry

Here are the top 10 companies in the automation industry

  1. ABB


    A Switzerland based multinational firm that serves a varied range of industries with automation products, system and various services with its operations in more than 100 nations. Mainly known for its manufacturing robotics, ABB has been in the market since 1988 allowing innovative industrial automation solutions to process and hybrid businesses. Their contributions involve industry-specific integrated automation, software and excellent services, electrification and digital solutions, control technologies, measurement & analytics, and marine & turbocharging offerings. ABB’s Industrial Automation industry is writing the future of safe and smart operations by helping its customers survive competitive, improving their ROI and running safe and productive processes.


  2. Rockwell Automation


    Despite acquisition efforts from Emmerson few years back, Rockwell Automation maintains to be one of the top industrial automation companies over the globe. Headquartered in Milwaukee it crosses over 80 countries across the globe focusing on technology change, field expertise, and integrity and corporate responsibility. Although greatly famous for its flagship brands Allen-Bradley® and Rockwell Software®, the company works in the Architecture and Software, and Control Products and Solutions segments.


  3. Siemens


    Siemens is a German multinational conglomerate organisation headquartered in Munich focusing on the domains of automation, digitalization, and electrification. Company is one of the world’s biggest producers of energy-efficient, resource-saving technologies. Siemens’ technological expertise and long-standing experience are serving to pioneer a sustainable tomorrow across the globe. Aside from its Digital Factory and Process Industries, Siemens has given a big commitment to the automation and manufacturing areas by embedding its MindSphere cloud-based, the open operating system for IIoT.


  4. Schneider Electric


    Mainly in energy management and automation industry Schneider Electric caters manufacturing industry including a great product selection for Homes, Buildings, Data Centers, Infrastructure and Industries. EcoStruxure, an IoT-enabled, plug-and-play, open, interoperable architecture and platform is company’s flagship product that delivers Innovation at Each Level from Connected Products to Edge Control, and Apps, Analytics and Services, on 6 areas of expertise - Power, IT, Building, Machine, Plant and Grid - delivering improved value about safety, reliability, operational efficiency, sustainability, and connectivity to clients.


  5. Honeywell


    Honeywell, a US-based MNC that reaches across geographies with an extensive product selection across different industries. Broadly operating in four strategic units -Honeywell Aerospace, Safety and Productivity Solutions (SPS), Home and Building Technologies (HBT) and Honeywell Performance Materials & Technologies. Honeywell demonstrates a big commitment to its customers. Honeywell is leveraging shift of industries towards digital change with its Connected Plant program that integrates processes, assets and people to help clients digitalize their legacy hardware to software, particularly its large base of TDC3000 DCS customers.


  6. Emerson Process Management


    Emerson Process Management is an American Multi-national company concentrates on electrical equipment. Emerson provides its services to industrial, commercial and consumer businesses. This company provides automation services to empower businesses to thrive and develop the industries. This company’s automation results collaborate with automation businesses to form more complete solutions for customers based on industry knowledge.


  7. Mitsubishi electric


    Mitsubishi Electric is a Japanese electrical and electronics equipment manufacturing multinational firm. Mitsubishi Electric utilizes its superior engineering techniques to provide reliable FA results with an eye to the next generation of manufacturing. The company has been a supplier to many other industrialisation companies varying from TVs to fighter jets.


  8. Yokogawa electric


    Yokogawa Electric is a principal provider of Industrial Automation and Test and Measurement Solutions. This Japanese electrical and software-based company combines excellent technology with engineering assistance, project administration, and maintenance. Yokogawa serves field-proven operational performance, safety, quality, and reliability.


  9. Omron automation


    Omron automation was founded 80 years ago and it is one of the top automation companies in the world. The major interest of the company is the manufacture and sale of automated equipment and systems.This company allows sensors, switches, safety elements, Relays, control components and so on.


  10. Danaher Industrial Ltd


    Danaher Industrial Ltd offers design for industrial and consumer products and manufactures them. It proposes services in the field of Life science, Industries, environmental, Testing and control plus several others.
